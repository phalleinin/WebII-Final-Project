About Us

Discover Our Story and What We Offer

Our Story

Pisa was created to celebrate the rich culinary traditions of Cambodia. Our passion for authentic Khmer cuisine drives us to serve dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients that honor the flavors of our culture.

At Pisa, every meal tells a story—whether it’s a comforting bowl of Khmer noodle soup or a vibrant plate of fish amok. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it’s a gathering space where friends, families, and travelers come together to savor the true taste of Cambodia.

We strive to deliver an unforgettable dining experience through traditional flavors and exceptional hospitality.

Our Mission

  • Provide authentic Cambodian dishes
  • Celebrate Khmer culinary heritage
  • Deliver exceptional dining experiences
  • Foster a warm and welcoming atmosphere

Our Services

  • Dine-in Experience
  • Catering for Special Events
  • Takeaway & Delivery Options
  • Private Dining Reservations

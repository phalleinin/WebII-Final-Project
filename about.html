About Us

Get to know who we are and our offers

Our Story

Pi-Sa was born from a passion for Cambodian cuisine and a desire to help food lovers navigate the rich culinary landscape of Phnom Penh. What started as a simple idea has grown into a comprehensive platform dedicated to exploring, reviewing, and celebrating Khmer food.

Recognizing the incredible diversity and depth of Cambodian cuisine, we set out to create a one-stop destination for food enthusiasts, travelers, and local diners alike. Our mission is to shine a spotlight on the hidden gems and beloved eateries that make Phnom Penh's food scene truly special.

Pi-Sa is more than just a restaurant – it's a culinary journey through the heart of Cambodian gastronomy.

Our Mission

  • Discover the best Khmer dishes
  • Provide the best ingredients
  • Connect diners with local eateries
  • Simplify restaurant reservations
  • Celebrate Cambodian culinary culture

Our Services

  • Detailed dining reviews
  • Easy online reservations
  • Curated dining recommendations
  • Local food discovery platform
